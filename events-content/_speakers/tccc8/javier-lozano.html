--- speakerId: JavierLozano name: Javier Lozano image: speakerUrl: twitter: github: event: tccc8 --- Javier Lozano is an ASP.NET Insider and MVP that specializes in ASP.NET, system architecture, and training. He has helped co-found the Iowa .NET Users Group as well as the Iowa Code Camp. On his blog, http://blog.lozanotek.com , you can find posts on ASP.NET, Silverlight, architecture, and design patterns just to name a few topics. Javier is an avid supporter of the community and likes to give back by speaking at user groups, local/regional .NET events, participating in forums, and by writing articles. In his spare time, Javier loves spending time with his family and enjoys writing about himself in the third person.
