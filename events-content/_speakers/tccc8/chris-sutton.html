--- speakerId: ChrisSutton name: Chris Sutton image: speakerUrl: twitter: github: event: tccc8 --- Chris Sutton is a software developer and technical trainer in Eastern Iowa. He helps run CRineta.org in Cedar Rapids, Iowa and he co-founded the Iowa Code Camp in 2008. Chris is a Microsoft MVP, a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) and holds the MCPD:Enterprise certification. He has been consulting and speaking on web related topics since the beginning of 2005 and loves developing for the web. He also enjoys hiking and biking in the summer with his family and snowshoeing in the winter.
