--- speakerId: ScottDavis name: Scott Davis image: speakerUrl: twitter: github: event: tccc8 --- Scott Davis is a seasoned consultant with a decade of consulting experience on both the Microsoft and Java platforms, involving mostly Web application development. Recent work for Scott has primarily revolved around Microsoft Silverlight, starting with the 1.1 Alpha release. Scott has experience in web, desktop, mobility, reporting, and database design and development, as well as project & staff management. Scott is currently the owner and principal consultant for Ignition Point Solutions; he is the leader of the Twin Cities Silverlight User Group; he is the president of BizLounge, a local entrepreneur group; and he is a member of the adjunct faculty in the information technology management master's degree program at St Mary's University in Minneapolis.
