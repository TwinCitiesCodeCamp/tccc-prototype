--- event: tccc20 title: "The Why and How of Dependency Injection" speaker: SeanMacAvaney layout: talk room: Rapson 43 time: 1:00 p.m. --- Many frameworks use dependency injection to improve testability and allow for runtime dependency configuration. In this session we further discuss the motivation of this approach and implement a service locator in an ASP.NET MVC project. Related topics covered along the way include inversion of control and reflection.
