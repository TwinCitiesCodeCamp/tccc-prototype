--- event: tccc22 title: "Secure Coding" speaker: MarkKalal layout: talk room: Garden Room time: 9:15 a.m. --- Information security involves keeping your systems secure and your company's (and your users) data safe and out of unauthorized hands. We as developers have a number of very important roles in info sec, and one of those is secure coding. In this presentation we'll talk about some secure coding practices, examples of vulnerabilities, and practical ways to avoid doing the sort of things that the bad guys exploit.
