--- event: tccc23 title: "Agile Team Member: The key to real delivery value" speaker: DarrellAndersen layout: talk room: P0838 time: 3:00 p.m. tags: ["agile", "team building"] --- Some Agile teams have Product Owners, some have Scrum Masters, but all of them have Team Members. While ceremonies are important, prioritizing of work is helpful and a daily standup never hurts, but without team members, nothing gets done. In this session we will highlight the key delivery aspects than an agile team member is responsible for and you will leave with the top five things you can do on your agile team to maximize delivery value.
