Hogan is a web developer, editor, educator, and author of 10 technical books, including Small, Sharp Software Tools, Exercises For Programmers and tmux 2: Productive Mouse-Driven Development. He currently works at DigitalOcean, where he helps people learn how to deploy applications to the cloud. When he's not working, he's hacking on code, composing music, or spending time with family and friends.
