--- event: tccc24 title: "Kicked Upstairs - Career Advice from a Techie Turned Manager" speakerId: BrianSeebacher layout: talk room: time: tags: ["career development", "career building", "team building"] --- There are many paths a career in technology career can take, and not all of them involve computers. Keeping up to date on the latest technology trends while learning how to lead teams, address the needs of users, and communicate effectively with stakeholders can be a difficult balancing act. In this talk we'll share some hard won knowledge from years of dealing with both machines and people, and what happens as your role evolves.
