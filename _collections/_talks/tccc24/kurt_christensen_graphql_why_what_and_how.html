--- event: tccc24 title: "GraphQL: Why, What and How" speaker: KurtChristensen layout: talk room: time: tags: ["graphql", "clojure", "aws"] --- GraphQL offers a compelling alternative to REST for services which have multiple clients, or clients with frequently changing needs. In this session, Kurt Christensen will show the motivation for GraphQL, and how to implement a GraphQL API using Clojure and the Lacinia GraphQL library.
