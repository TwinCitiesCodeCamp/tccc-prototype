--- event: tccc24 title: "Should You Be the Boss?" speakerId: DanNordquist layout: talk room: time: tags: ["career development"] --- Programming careers can be unpredictable, but at some point, you'll probably be confronted by the idea of becoming a manager. Management jobs can vary widely, though, and it can be hard to know if being the boss is something you'd even like. Join Dan Nordquist as he walks through what he knows about leadership, "career tracks", promotions, teams, and making one of the most important decisions in your life.
